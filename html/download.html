Download

Please feel free to download the software and tell us about your expericences and problems you may encounter. Make sure you understand the license. Parts of the software (Telnet Protocol Handler, Terminal Emulation) are available under the GNU Library Public License which is not quite as restrictive as the GPL. If you are still having problems with the license please do not hesitate to contact us.

For some plugins you may need extra packages. You can download the archives from the box on the right side of your browser window. Also have a look at the documentation before asking for help.
